## 📌 About the Project

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to understand revenue trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior across different time periods.

The analysis was performed using Excel for data cleaning and transformation, and Tableau was used to build an interactive dashboard for visualization and insights generation.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement

The coffee shop wanted to understand:
- Which coffee products generate the highest revenue
- Sales trends across different time periods (hour/day/month)
- Customer payment preferences (cash vs card)
- Peak business hours and sales patterns
- Overall revenue performance and growth trends

---

## ⚙️ Process

### 1. Data Cleaning (Excel)
- Removed duplicates and missing values
- Standardized coffee product names
- Corrected inconsistent formatting in date and time fields

### 2. Data Transformation
- Created calculated fields such as:
  - Total Revenue
  - Hour of purchase categories (Morning, Afternoon, Evening)
  - Day-wise and month-wise grouping
- Structured dataset for dashboard readiness

### 3. Data Visualization (Tableau)
- Built interactive dashboard with filters and slicers
- Created KPI cards for total revenue and orders
- Designed charts for:
  - Sales by coffee type
  - Revenue trends over time
  - Peak sales hours analysis

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

- **Latte and Americano** were among the top revenue-generating coffee types
- Sales were highest during **morning and afternoon hours**
- **Card payments** were more commonly used than Cash
- Certain weekdays showed higher sales activity compared to weekends
- Revenue showed consistent patterns with noticeable peak hours during business times
